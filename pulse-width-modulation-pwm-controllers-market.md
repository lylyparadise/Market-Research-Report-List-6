<p><p>The Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) controllers market is primarily categorized into Current Mode and Voltage Mode PWM controllers. Current Mode PWM controllers regulate output by adjusting the duty cycle based on current feedback, providing fast response and inherent overcurrent protection. In contrast, Voltage Mode PWM controllers adjust the duty cycle based on output voltage feedback, offering simplicity and stability but with slower transient response. These distinct types cater to varying application needs in power supply management and energy efficiency.</p></p>
