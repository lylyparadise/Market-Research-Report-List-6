<p><p>Carsharing can be categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Roundtrip**: Users pick up a vehicle from one location and return it to the same spot. </p><p>2. **One-way**: Vehicles can be picked up at one location and dropped off at another, providing flexibility for users. </p><p>3. **Peer-to-peer**: Individuals can share their personal cars with others through a platform, promoting community sharing. </p><p>4. **Fractional market**: Users purchase shares in a vehicle, allowing them access to it for a specified time without full ownership.</p></p>
