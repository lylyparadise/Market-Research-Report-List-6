<p><ul><li>Powder SOP</li><li>Granular SOP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sulfate of potash (SOP) market is primarily divided into two types: powder SOP and granular SOP. Powder SOP is finely ground and dissolves quickly in water, making it ideal for foliar applications and operations requiring rapid nutrient uptake. In contrast, granular SOP consists of larger particles that dissolve more slowly, providing a sustained nutrient release ideal for bulk application in soil. Each form caters to different agricultural practices and crop needs, influencing distribution and pricing in the market.</p></p>
