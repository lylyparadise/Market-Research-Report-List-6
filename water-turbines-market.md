<p><strong>The Water Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Water Turbines</li><li>Reactive Water Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water turbines market primarily comprises two types: impulse and reactive turbines. Impulse water turbines harness energy from high-velocity water jets, converting kinetic energy into mechanical energy, commonly found in run-of-river hydro systems. Reactive water turbines, on the other hand, rely on the pressure and flow of water, using both kinetic and potential energy. These turbines are typically used in larger hydroelectric plants, where water pressure is significant. Both types serve to produce renewable energy efficiently.</p></p>
