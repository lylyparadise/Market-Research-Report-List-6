<p><strong>The Angle Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Incremental Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angle encoders market is primarily divided into two types: absolute encoders and incremental encoders. Absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each angle, ensuring precise and unambiguous readings, which is vital for applications requiring high accuracy. In contrast, incremental encoders generate relative position data, measuring changes in position, making them suitable for speed detection and simpler applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including robotics, automation, and manufacturing, where accurate angle measurement is critical.</p></p>
