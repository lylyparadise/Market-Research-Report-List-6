<p><p>The RNA Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) market includes various types of methodologies tailored for genomic analysis. Enrichment approaches focus on isolating specific RNA sequences of interest, improving the detection of low-abundance transcripts. In contrast, amplicon-based approaches amplify specific target regions, allowing for precise sequencing of chosen genes or regions. Both methods enhance the sensitivity and specificity of RNA sequencing, facilitating advanced research in genomics, transcriptomics, and personalized medicine by enabling deeper insights into gene expression and variations.</p></p>
