<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Review System consists of two market types: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method focuses on comparing new mask designs directly with existing masks from similar dies, ensuring quality by examining specific physical attributes and functionality. In contrast, the DB method involves assessing new masks against a comprehensive database of mask metrics, leveraging historical data and established benchmarks for analysis. Both methods enhance the efficiency and accuracy of mask verification in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
