<p><p>The Outdoor Air Quality Monitoring System market comprises several types of monitoring approaches. Active or continuous monitoring involves real-time data collection using automated instruments, ensuring constant air quality assessment. Passive monitoring relies on absorbent materials to collect pollutants over time without power. Intermittent monitoring involves periodic checks to gather data at specific intervals. Manual monitoring requires human intervention to take readings, while stack monitoring focuses on assessing emissions from industrial chimneys, providing insights into pollution from specific sources.</p></p>
