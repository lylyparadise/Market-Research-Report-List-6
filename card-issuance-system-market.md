<p><p>The card issuance system market consists of two main types: Central Card Issuance Systems and Card Instant Issuance Systems. Central Card Issuance Systems enable banks and financial institutions to produce and manage a large volume of cards centrally, often leading to cost efficiencies and streamlined operations. In contrast, Card Instant Issuance Systems allow for immediate production and delivery of cards at branch locations, enhancing customer experience by providing instant access to card services. Both types serve distinct operational needs in the financial sector.</p></p>
