<p><p>The aircraft brake calipers market comprises three main types: single piston, double piston, and multiple piston brake caliper types. Single piston calipers utilize one piston to engage the brake pads, providing a simple yet effective braking solution. Double piston calipers have two pistons for enhanced force and responsiveness, improving braking performance. Multiple piston calipers, featuring various pistons, offer superior control and uniform pressure distribution, making them ideal for larger aircraft. Each type caters to specific performance and weight requirements in aviation applications.</p></p>
