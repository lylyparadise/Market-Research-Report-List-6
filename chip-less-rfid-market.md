<p><strong>The Chip-less RFID Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW</li><li>TFTC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip-less RFID technology utilizes passive RFID tags that don't contain integrated circuits. The two main market types are Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Time-Frequency Tagging Communication (TFTC). SAW technology relies on surface acoustic waves to transmit data, making it suitable for high-frequency applications like inventory tracking and item authentication. Conversely, TFTC uses varying frequencies to encode information, enabling it to function efficiently in dynamic environments, such as supply chain management and logistics. Both markets emphasize cost-effectiveness and scalability.</p></p>
