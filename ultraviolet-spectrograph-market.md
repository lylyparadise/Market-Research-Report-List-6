<p><strong>The Ultraviolet Spectrograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Beam</li><li>Double-Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet Spectrograph market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Beam and Double-Beam. Single-Beam spectrographs measure the intensity of UV light from a sample by comparing it to a baseline measurement, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Double-Beam spectrographs, on the other hand, split the light into two beams for simultaneous measurement of sample and reference, improving accuracy and stability by compensating for fluctuations in the light source. This distinction influences application areas, pricing, and user preferences in the market.</p></p>
