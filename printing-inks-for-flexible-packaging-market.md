<p><p>Printing inks for flexible packaging are categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and UV-curable inks. Water-based inks utilize water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for various substrates. Solvent-based inks employ organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and durability but may pose environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. UV-curable inks are quick-drying and use ultraviolet light for curing, providing high gloss and durability, making them ideal for vibrant and long-lasting packaging applications.</p></p>
