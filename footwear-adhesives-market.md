<p><p>The footwear adhesives market comprises various types tailored for different applications. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding but can emit volatile organic compounds. Water-based adhesives are more environmentally friendly, suitable for lighter materials. Radiation-cured adhesives, like UV or electron beam, provide fast curing times and excellent durability. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic, offering quick application and strong adhesion when cooled. Each type is designed to meet specific performance and environmental requirements in footwear manufacturing.</p></p>
