<p><p>The Electro-Optical and Infrared Sensor market can be categorized into hyperspectral and multispectral sensors. Hyperspectral sensors capture a wide range of wavelengths across the electromagnetic spectrum, enabling detailed spectral analysis and object identification for applications like agricultural monitoring and environmental assessments. In contrast, multispectral sensors focus on fewer specific wavelength bands, typically capturing data in three to ten bands. These sensors are commonly used in industries such as defense, agriculture, and forestry for tasks like surveillance and crop health evaluation.</p></p>
