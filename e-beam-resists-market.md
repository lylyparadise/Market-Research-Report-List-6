<p><strong>The E-beam Resists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive E-beam Resists</li><li>Negative E-beam Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-beam resists market is categorized into positive and negative types based on their chemical response to electron beam exposure. Positive E-beam resists become soluble upon exposure, allowing for high-resolution imaging and fine patterning in applications like semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, negative E-beam resists become insoluble when exposed, enabling the creation of features where the resist remains after development. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of resolution, contrast, and etch resistance, catering to varied needs in microfabrication processes.</p></p>
