<p><p>The overhead and underground conductor market includes various types of conductors used for electrical transmission. AAC (All Aluminium Conductor) consists solely of aluminium, offering high conductivity. AAAC (All Aluminium Alloy Conductor) combines aluminium with alloy, improving strength and reducing sag. ACSR (Aluminium Conductor, Steel Reinforced) features a steel core for enhanced tensile strength, making it suitable for long spans. ACAR (Aluminium Conductor, Alloy Reinforced) offers a blend of aluminium and alloy for both durability and lightweight properties. The "Others" category encompasses specialized or less common conductor types.</p></p>
