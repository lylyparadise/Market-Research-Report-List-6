<p><p>The automotive fuel pump module market is primarily divided into two types: electric fuel pump modules and mechanical fuel pump modules. Electric fuel pump modules use an electric motor to draw fuel from the tank and deliver it to the engine, offering efficiency and reliability in modern vehicles. In contrast, mechanical fuel pump modules operate through an engine-driven mechanism, traditionally used in older vehicles and simpler systems. Both types play critical roles in fuel delivery, impacting vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
