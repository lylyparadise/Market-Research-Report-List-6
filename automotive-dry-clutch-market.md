<p><strong>The Automotive Dry Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Disc Dry Clutch</li><li>Multi-disc Dry Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dry clutch market is primarily categorized into single-disc and multi-disc types. Single-disc dry clutches are widely used in smaller vehicles for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, providing reliable engagement and disengagement of the powertrain. In contrast, multi-disc dry clutches employ multiple friction surfaces, allowing for higher torque capacity and better heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance applications such as sports cars and heavy-duty vehicles. Both types are essential for optimizing vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
