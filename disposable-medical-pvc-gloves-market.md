<p><p>The Disposable Medical PVC Gloves market is categorized into two main types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a starch-based powder, making them easier to put on and remove, which enhances dexterity during procedures. However, they may carry a risk of allergic reactions and contamination. In contrast, non-powdered gloves do not contain any additives, reducing the risk of allergens and contamination, but they may be slightly more difficult to don. Both types cater to different user preferences and clinical needs.</p></p>
