<p><p>The fibrotic bronchoscopy market is primarily categorized into rigid and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes are characterized by their sturdy design, offering superior visibility and access for invasive procedures, making them ideal for larger airways. In contrast, flexible bronchoscopes are more versatile and can navigate complex bronchial structures, allowing for diagnostic and therapeutic interventions in both adults and children. The choice between these types depends on the clinical needs, procedure type, and patient condition, driving their demand in healthcare settings.</p></p>
