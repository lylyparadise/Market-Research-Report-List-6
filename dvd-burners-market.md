<p><p>DVD burners are categorized into four market types based on storage capacity and design. Single-Sided Single-Layer DVDs hold about 4.7 GB of data, ideal for basic storage needs. Single-Sided Double-Layer DVDs double this capacity to approximately 8.5 GB on one side. Double-Sided Single Layer DVDs offer two 4.7 GB sides, totaling 9.4 GB but requiring manual flipping. Finally, Double-Sided Double-Layer DVDs provide up to 17 GB with two sides of increased capacity, catering to users needing extensive storage in a compact format.</p></p>
