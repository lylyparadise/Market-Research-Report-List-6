<p><strong>The Oilfield Thread Metal Protectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pin Protectors</li><li>Box Protectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oilfield thread metal protectors market is primarily categorized into two types: pin protectors and box protectors. Pin protectors safeguard the exposed threads on the drilling pipe's end, preventing damage during transportation and storage. Box protectors, on the other hand, cover the internal threads of pipe connections, ensuring they remain intact and free from contamination. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity of drilling equipment, reducing wear and tear, and enhancing operational efficiency in oilfield operations.</p></p>
