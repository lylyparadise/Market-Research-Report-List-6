<p><p>Distributed Vibration Sensing (DVS) technology is categorized into two main market types: Multi-Mode and Single-Mode systems. Multi-Mode DVS utilizes multiple fiber optic modes to detect vibrations over extended distances, offering higher sensitivity and the ability to monitor diverse applications simultaneously. In contrast, Single-Mode DVS employs a single light mode, enabling precise sensing with lower loss over longer distances, ideal for critical security and infrastructure monitoring. Both types serve specialized needs within the growing DVS market across various industries.</p></p>
