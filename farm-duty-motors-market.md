<p><ul><li>Fractional Farm Duty Motors</li><li>Integral Farm Duty Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Farm duty motors are categorized into fractional and integral types based on their power output and application. Fractional farm duty motors typically have a power rating below one horsepower and are used for light-duty tasks, such as small pumps and fans. In contrast, integral farm duty motors have a power rating of one horsepower or more, suitable for heavier applications like irrigation systems and larger agricultural equipment. Both types are designed to withstand the demanding conditions of farm environments.</p></p>
