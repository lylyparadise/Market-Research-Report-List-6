Dry sand blasting machines use compressed air to propel abrasive material, effectively removing rust and contaminants. In contrast, wet sand blasting machines mix water with the abrasive, reducing airborne dust and providing a cleaner operation. Both types cater to different applications and preferences, enhancing efficiency and achieving desired surface finishes.</p></p>
