<p><p>The coating starches market includes several types, each with unique properties. Oxidized starch is used for its improved solubility and film-forming ability, commonly in food and pharmaceutical applications. Cross-linked starch offers enhanced stability and resistance to heat and shear, making it ideal for various industrial applications. Hydroxyalkyl starch provides excellent water solubility and thickening properties, often utilized in cosmetics and food products. Porous starch is valued for its ability to absorb oils and flavors, enhancing food texture and quality.</p></p>
