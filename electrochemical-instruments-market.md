<p><p>The electrochemical instruments market encompasses various techniques for measuring and analyzing electrochemical processes. Potentiometry involves measuring voltage to determine ion concentration, often used in pH meters. Voltammetry measures current as a function of voltage, providing insights into the redox behavior of analytes. Coulometry quantifies the total electric charge passed to determine substance concentration. Other methods may include impedimetric and conductometric techniques. Together, these approaches cater to different applications across industries like pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p></p>
