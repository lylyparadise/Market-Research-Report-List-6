<p><p>The wave soldering system market comprises two primary types: fully wave soldering systems and semi-automatic wave soldering systems. Fully wave soldering systems are automated machines designed for high-volume production, offering efficiency and consistency, ideal for large manufacturing operations. In contrast, semi-automatic wave soldering systems combine manual input with automated features, catering to smaller production runs or specific applications.
