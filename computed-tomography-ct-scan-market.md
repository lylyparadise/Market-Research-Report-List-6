<p><ul><li>X-Ray Computed Tomography (CT)</li><li>Ultrasonic Computed Tomography (CT)</li><li>Gamma Rays CT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Computed Tomography (CT) scan market includes several types: X-Ray CT, Ultrasonic CT, and Gamma Rays CT. X-Ray CT is the most common, utilizing X-ray technology to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body. Ultrasonic CT employs sound waves to produce images, offering a non-invasive and radiation-free alternative. Gamma Rays CT uses gamma radiation to visualize internal structures, particularly in nuclear medicine, providing insights into metabolic processes. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes and contributes to advancements in medical imaging.</p></p>
