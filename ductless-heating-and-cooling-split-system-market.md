<p><p>Ductless heating and cooling split systems include three main market types: Mini Split Systems, which consist of a single indoor and outdoor unit, ideal for small spaces; Multi Split Systems, accommodating multiple indoor units connected to one outdoor unit, suitable for larger areas; and VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) Systems, designed for simultaneous heating and cooling across multiple zones, providing enhanced energy efficiency and comfort. These systems offer flexible installation options and individual climate control without the need for ductwork.</p></p>
