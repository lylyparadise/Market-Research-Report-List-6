<p><p>The photomask review machine market is segmented into Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB) methods. The DD method entails comparing a physical die against a known good die, ensuring quality and precision in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, the DB method involves comparing the die to a pre-established database of verified designs, enabling faster fault detection and analysis. Both methods are essential for maintaining high standards in mask production and enhancing the reliability of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
