<p><p>The ultrasound devices market is categorized into several types: 2D, 3D, and 4D ultrasound systems, along with Doppler ultrasound. 2D ultrasound provides flat images and is primarily used for basic diagnostic purposes. 3D ultrasound captures three-dimensional images, enhancing visualization of structures, while 4D ultrasound adds real-time motion to 3D images. Doppler ultrasound assesses blood flow and detects vascular conditions. Collectively, these technologies offer diverse applications in prenatal imaging, cardiology, and various medical diagnostics, driving market growth.</p></p>
