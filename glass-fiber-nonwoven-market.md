<p><strong>The Glass FIber Nonwoven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Nonwoven Market is primarily categorized into two types based on their manufacturing processes: Wet-Laid and Dry-Laid. The Wet-Laid process involves suspending glass fibers in water, creating a mat that is then dried and consolidated. This method offers superior uniformity and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring high durability. Conversely, the Dry-Laid process involves layering dry glass fibers, followed by mechanical bonding. This technique is generally faster and more cost-effective, suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
