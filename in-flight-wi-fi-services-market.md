<p><strong>The In-Flight Wi-Fi Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-to-ground technology</li><li>Satellite technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-flight Wi-Fi services primarily utilize two technologies: air-to-ground and satellite. Air-to-ground technology connects aircraft to ground-based cellular networks, enabling internet access through a series of antennas on the ground. This method is often more cost-effective and provides high-speed connectivity over regions with dense population. In contrast, satellite technology uses satellites orbiting the Earth to provide internet connectivity, offering coverage over vast and remote areas, making it suitable for transoceanic flights but often with higher latency and cost.</p></p>
