<p><p>Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) devices are categorized into two main types: Fixed Pressure BiPAP devices and Auto Adjusting BiPAP devices. Fixed Pressure BiPAP devices deliver a constant pressure during inhalation and a lower pressure during exhalation, providing steady support for patients with respiratory issues. In contrast, Auto Adjusting BiPAP devices automatically adjust the pressure based on the patient's breathing patterns, offering tailored support that enhances comfort and effectiveness. Both types are vital for managing conditions like sleep apnea and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).</p></p>
