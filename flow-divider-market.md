<p><p>Flow dividers are crucial components in fluid power systems, categorized primarily into volumetric flow and mass flow types. Volumetric flow dividers allocate fluid based on set volumes, ensuring balanced distribution in hydraulic systems. In contrast, mass flow dividers measure and divide the mass of fluids, accounting for variations in density, making them ideal for precise applications. Both types are essential for optimizing performance, enhancing efficiency, and ensuring consistent operation in industrial machinery and equipment.</p></p>
