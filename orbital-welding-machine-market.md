<p><p>The orbital welding machine market includes various types based on welding processes. Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW) utilizes a non-consumable tungsten electrode, offering high precision for thin materials. Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW) employs a continuous wire feed for faster operations and versatility, suitable for various metals. Flux Core Arc Welding (FCAW) uses a tubular wire filled with flux, providing strong welds in outdoor settings. Other types encompass alternative methods like Resistance Welding and Laser Welding, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
